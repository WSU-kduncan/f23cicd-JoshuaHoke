From ancient times to the modern-day, bonsai is a beautiful art form and a student of bonsai learns much about nature, gaining a deep sense of satisfaction from its study and practice. You will learn a lot about plants and trees, flowers, seeds, leaves, varieties of wood and soil, and many other aspects of the agricultural and horticultural sciences. Additionally, being a student at Royal Bonsai Society organizes educational trips to various centers of bonsai activity throughout the United States and throughout the world!

Remember:

Bonsai study, practice and cultivation can be done indoors in your home or greenhouse, as well as outdoors in many different locations and environments. People with health or other conditions need not feel prohibited from practicing the ancient art form of bonsai! Bonsai is a healthy practice for any individual, younger, older, or anywhere in between! Bonsai is needed by our modern-world society more than ever, benefitting all through its pacifying, calming, as well as aesthetic and intellectual effects on people and society, and also with the natural beautification it creates in any landscape within which it is visible. In no time at all, you'll be the envy of every other gardener in your neighborhood!
